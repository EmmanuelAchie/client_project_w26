Job/Intership Searching

Finding the Right Career Path for you

Finding the right internship or job can feel overwhelming for graduate students navigating a competitive tech market. This is a great place to start your job and internship search by bringing together curated opportunities, career resources, and practical guidance in one place. This platform is designed to help you explore various career paths, connect with potential employers, and access tools to enhance your job search strategies.

Job & Internship Discovery

  • Browse UX/UI & Tech Internships: Explore a curated list of internships in UX/UI, data analysis, and web development that match graduate students’ skills and career goals.
  • Lastest Entry-Level Job Openings: Stay up to date with newly posted entry-level positions in technology and design fields, all in one easy-to-search place.
  • Company Spotlights: Discover featured companies actively recruiting students and recent graduates, along with insights into their culture and career paths.

Skill Development

  • Top Skills Employers Want: Learn which technical and soft skills are most in demand and how to prioritize them in your learning and job search.
  • Project & Portfolio Practices Ideas: Find guided project prompts to help you apply your skills in real-world scenarios and build a strong professional portfolio.
  • Skill Gap Checker: Compare your current skills with job requirements to identify areas for growth and create a focused learning plan.
  • Tools & Software Guides: Get familiar with popular tools like Figma, Tableau, Python, and Git through beginner-friendly walkthroughs and tips.

Organization & Planning

  • Weekly Job Search Planner: Stay organized with a simple planner that helps you schedule applications, networking, and skill-building tasks each week.
  • Goal Setting for Your Career Path: Set clear short-term and long-term career goals to keep your job search focused and aligned with your interests in UX, data, or web development.
  • Saved Jobs and Personalized Recommendations: Save opportunities you’re interested in and receive tailored job and internship suggestions based on your skills and preferences.

Job/Internship FAQ

  • How do I start searching for internships or jobs in UX, data analysis, or web development?
    Begin by identifying your career interests and building a basic resume and portfolio, then use job boards, company websites, and networking platforms like UMSI CareerLink and LinkedIn to find relevant opportunities.
  • When should graduate students begin applying for internships or full-time roles?
    Graduate students should start applying at least 3–6 months before their desired start date, as many companies recruit early and have long hiring timelines.
  • What skills do employers look for in entry-level tech and design positions?
    Employers seek a mix of technical skills (such as design tools, coding, or data analysis software) and soft skills like communication, problem-solving, and teamwork.
  • How should I tailor my resume for UX or data roles?
    Customize your resume by emphasizing relevant projects, technical tools (such as Figma, Python, or SQL), and measurable outcomes that match the job description.
  • How long does the hiring process usually take for graduate students
    The hiring process can take anywhere from a few weeks to several months, depending on the company, number of applicants, and interview stages.
